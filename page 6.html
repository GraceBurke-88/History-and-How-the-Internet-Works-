Top-level domain ICANN "Internet Corporation For Assigned Names and Numbers." ICANN is a not for profit corporation that is in charge of managing Domain Names and delineating who manages IP Addresses. ICANN is based in the U.S. but manages the entire world They oversee “over 180 million domain names and four billion network addresses across 240 countries” ICANN helps to keep the internet open and not controlled by any one person, business, or country.
